“Most Reliable Partner Award” – Infosys Partner Meet 2016

Inspirisys’s service excellence and commitment to service delivery in the enterprise application space (Banking) was recognized by core banking platform provider – INFOSYS, in their global partner meet held in Mysore, India on the 17th of October 2016.

The award was presented by Ms. Sheenam Ohrie – head of Global Delivery and was accepted by Mr. P Manoharan – Vice President Banking Division - on behalf of Inspirisys’s Banking Services team.

Under Mr. Manoharan’s able leadership and supported by a core group of Industry experts and technical consultants, this team has consistently delivered projects on-time and within budget; but never at the cost of compromising quality.

The award acknowledged Inspirisys’s commitment to consistent service delivery and service excellence, while making special mention of the scale of operations of the Banking Practice and mostly importantly, its customer centricity!

Let us join in celebrating Inspirisys’s banking practice’s success and here’s wishing them many more such accolades to come!
